Privacy Policy for TrackHW

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

TrackHW ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our homework tracking services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google User Data: If you link your Google account, we access your email address (see below for more information), as well as your Google Classroom classes and assignments to sync with your dashboard. We do not edit any Google data.
  • Account Info: We store your name and email address provided during login to identify your account.

3. Use of Data (Google Limited Use Disclosure)

TrackHW's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We use your Google data only to provide user-facing features (syncing assignments). We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Users can disconnect their Google account at any time. Upon request, we will delete all stored user data within 30 days. Contact us at support@trackhw.com.
